Career

My career journey has involved leadership roles for national and international observatories and projects in the Americas and Europe. I have served as an academic dean for a sciences college and university professor.

I have been priviledged to work with many of the leading scientists, engineers, and administrators from the astronomical communities in the Americas, Europe, Australia, East Asia, and South Asia.

Recently, I completed 5-years as an academic dean at the University of Texas at San Antonio. I am currently serving as a senior professor with engagement in physics, astronomy, data science, and space sector technology.
